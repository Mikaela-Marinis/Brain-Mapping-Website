About the Contributers

    This website was created my Craig Haber, a double major in psychology and computer science at Connecticut College, and Mikaela Marinis, a computer science major at the same school. All content was reviewed by the neuroscientist Dr. Joseph Schroeder for accuracy. In addition to Dr. Schroeder, a special thanks to Dr. Tarimo for his continuous guidance and educational technology expertise.
